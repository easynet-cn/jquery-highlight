背光,模组,背板,结构,后背板,组的后

本实用新型适用于背光模组领域，提供了一种背光模组的后背板结构，所述后背板由若干安装条互相拼接而成，各安装条之间为镂空结构。与现有的一体冲压成型的后背板结构相比，本实用新型采用若干安装条拼接而成后背板，解决了现有的因采用冲压工艺制造后背板而产生硬力导致扭曲变形的问题；另外，由于各安装条之间为镂空结构，在保证后背板强度的同时，既减轻了后背板的重量，又降低了后背板的成本，还提高了背光模组的散热效率。

back plate,plate structure,rear back,integrally formed,existing rear

The utility model is suitable for the field of back light modules, and provides a rear back plate structure of a back light module, wherein a rear back plate is formed through mutually splicing a plurality of mounting strips, and hollow out structures are arranged between various mounting strips. Compared with an existing rear back plate structure which is integrally formed, the rear back plate structure of the back light module uses a plurality of mounting strips to splice and form a rear back plate, thereby solving the problems that an existing rear back plate which is made through stamping process generates hard force to cause distortion and deformation. Furthermore, because the hollow out structures are arranged between various mounting strips, the strength of the rear back plate is guaranteed, the weight of the rear back plate is reduced at the same time, the cost of the rear back plate is also reduced, and the heat emission efficiency of the back light module is further increased.
